Scheduled Operations

Platform-level CronJobs that run automatically.

JobSchedulePurpose
CVE Rescan Configurable (default: daily) Re-scan fleet for new vulnerabilities
Compliance Rescan Monthly (1st, 3am UTC) Re-assess compliance posture across all apps
Dependency Update Weekly (Monday, 4am UTC) Check for outdated/vulnerable dependencies
Cost Report Weekly (Monday, 6am UTC) Collect resource usage, generate right-sizing recommendations
Pod/Pipeline Cleanup Every 30 minutes Delete completed/failed pods and old PipelineRuns
Data Retention Daily Purge events, jobs, and resolved gates older than {{ retention_days }} days
