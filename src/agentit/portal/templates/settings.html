Global Fallback Toggle

A coarse, whole-batch ON/OFF switch — everything the decision matrix below approves gets applied, with no per-(namespace, kind) scoping. Prefer the allowlist above for extending trust incrementally; use this only as a fallback (e.g. while you're still building out an allowlist) or to fully disable auto-mode. The LLM reviews every action either way — destructive operations are always gated for human approval regardless of this toggle.
